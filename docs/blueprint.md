# **App Name**: Zombie Corridor

## Core Features:

- First-Person Survival Engine: A high-performance FPS controller featuring W/S/A/D movement and precision mouse-look for immediate feedback.
- Endless Lockdown Generation: A procedural environment system that spawns endless corridors, doors, and facility props with a 'low-poly' aesthetic.
- Emergency Lighting Controller: Implements scrolling LED strip lighting, flickering emergency lamps, and crimson atmosphere to ensure constant visibility.
- AI Intensity Coordinator tool: An AI-powered tool that analyzes player distance and kill rate to dynamically scale zombie spawn frequency and difficulty level.
- Zombie Horde AI: Specialized pathfinding and behaviors for four distinct classes: Walker, Runner, Tank, and Elite zombies.
- Collapse Wave System: A persistent red energy barrier that advances behind the player, triggering screen pulses and warning flashes when proximity is critical.
- Survival Statistics HUD: Minimalist interface displaying HP, level, real-time distance tracking, and high score calculation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Emergency Crimson (#FF0000), a high-vibrancy red for warnings and moving LED strips.
- Background Color: Obsidian Depths (#140E0D), a deeply desaturated dark red that grounds the metallic corridor surfaces.
- Accent Color: Fuchsia Alert (#A30000), an analogous vibrant hue used for high-energy effects like the energy wave and screen pulses.
- Font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), chosen for its computerized, scientific aesthetic and high readability in high-stress game states.
- An immersive FPS perspective with an ultra-clean HUD layout, positioning survival metrics in the top corners to maintain visual focus on the zombies.
- Low-poly UI icons for health and distancing, matching the Minecraft-inspired geometric clarity of the environment models.
- High-velocity transitions for the red energy wall and rhythmic LED light-flowing effects to heighten the sense of forward urgency.
